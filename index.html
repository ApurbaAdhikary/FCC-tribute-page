Swami Vivekananda

Timeline of Swami Vivekananda's life:

  • 1863 - Narendranath Dutta. Born in Calcutta, India
  • 1877 - He was the only student to receive first-division marks in the Presidency College entrance examination.
  • 1884 - Completed a Bachelor of Arts Degree

    • Narendra is really a genius. I have travelled far and wide but I have never come across a lad of his talents and possibilities, even in German universities, among philosophical students. He is bound to make his mark in life

    William Hastie
  • 1884 - He started hiss spiritual journey with his guru Ramakrishna
  • 1886 - Narendranath took the name "Swami Vivekananda"
  • 1888 - Narendra left the monastery as a Parivrâjaka— the Hindu religious life of a wandering monk, "without fixed abode, without ties, independent and strangers wherever they go". His sole possessions were a kamandalu (water pot), staff and his two favourite books: the Bhagavad Gita and The Imitation of Christ
  • 1893 - Narendra left Bombay for Chicago with the name "Vivekananda", which means "the bliss of discerning wisdom," from Sanskrit viveka and ānanda

    • that man is simply a wonder for oratorical power. He is an honor to humanity.

    Harvard psychology professor William James
  • 1896 - His Book Raja Yoga was published, becoming an instant success; it was highly influential in western understanding of yoga, in Elizabeth, de Michelis's view marking the beginning of modern yoga
  • 1897 - Vivekananda founded Ramakrishna Mission in Calcutta
  • 1902 - Mysterious death(While doing meditation, mahasamadhi) at the age of 39
